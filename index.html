Inspired by a pop-culture phenomenon Star Wars, FarFarOut tells the story of two unknown Jedis who put themselves at life-threatening risk while fighting for peace in the Solar System.

Epic space battles happen so far yet so close to our precious colonies.

And that is where you come in.

Fire up those lasers and blast as many hostile spacecraft as you can because this fast-paced action game will not wait for you to warm up!

If you want to make it farther than we did in one of our favorite survival games, you will have to keep moving the space shuttle indeed fast to fire at the further enemies.

Game manual

How to control a spaceship?

You can fly to the left and to the right by pressing the LEFT and RIGHT ARROW keys respectively

How to shoot?

You can shoot and heartlessly kill all enemies on your way by pressing the SPACE key
