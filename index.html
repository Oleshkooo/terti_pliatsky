Once upon a time on the outskirts of the solar system...

There is unrest in the Galaxy
Several thousand star systems have announced their intention to capture all inhabited planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Ceres and Pluto
Two Jedi knights make a desperate attempt to protect the solar system from invaders...

Game manual

How to move?

You can scroll left and right by pressing the LEFT and RIGHT ARROW keys respectively

How to shoot?

You can shoot and heartlessly kill all enemies on your way by pressing the SPACE key
